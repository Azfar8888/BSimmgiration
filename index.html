About Us

About Us

At BSI Advisors, we offer professional UK immigration advice with a personal, client-focused approach. As a registered immigration adviser regulated by the Immigration Advice Authority (IAA), we specialise in Level 1 immigration matters, supporting individuals and families navigating the UK immigration system.

With a strong focus on accuracy, compliance, and clear communication, I aim to make your immigration journey easier to understand and less stressful. Whether you're exploring British citizenship, visa extensions, or settlement options, I’m here to guide you with reliable and honest advice.

  • Trusted & Regulated Expertise

    We are fully registered with the IAA and committed to providing reliable, compliant, and accurate immigration support tailored to your needs.

  • Clear & Honest Guidance

    Whether you're applying for British citizenship, a visa extension, or exploring settlement options, we’re here to guide you every step of the way with transparent, straightforward advice.

  • Independent & Confidential Service

    We are an independent advisory service—not affiliated with the UK Home Office or any government body. Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.

Our Services

Our ServiceS

We offer advice and document support for a range of straightforward UK immigration matters

British Citizenship (Naturalisation)

Guidance on the naturalisation process, including eligibility criteria and understanding required documentation.

Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)

Advice on ILR eligibility and the preparation of supporting documents for a smooth application process.

EU Settlement Scheme (Switch to Settled Status)

Support with switching from Pre-Settled to Settled Status, including understanding your timeline and document checklist.

Innovator Founder Visa – Document Preparation Support

We provide help in reviewing and organising supporting documents for entrepreneurs exploring the Innovator Founder route.

Visa Category Guidance (Extensions and Switches)

Information and support on available visa routes, including extending your stay or switching between eligible UK visa categories.

Spouse and Partner Visa Guidance

Advice and support for those joining or remaining with their partner in the UK under the family route.
